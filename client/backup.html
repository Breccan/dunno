Welcome to DiscoWorld

You can connect using the Browser Client or connect to dunno.no.de:8000 with your favourite Mud Client.

If you don't know what a MUD is go to wikipedia.

You can start doing stuff in the game using the following commands:

  • You can move around by typing a direction.
  • Look at things with 'look'
  • Talk with 'say' and 'tell'
